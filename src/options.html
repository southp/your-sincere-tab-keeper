ℹ️ About

Your Sincere Tab Keeper is designed to help you reflect on your browsing habits and build a more mindful relationship with technology.

Our Philosophy

We believe that small friction can lead to big changes. By adding a playful maze game when you exceed your tab limit, we help you pause and consider whether you really need that new tab.

Key Features

  • 🎯 Customizable tab limits (2-5)
  • 🧩 Progressive maze difficulty
  • 📊 Detailed usage statistics
  • 🔒 No bypass options (commitment to growth)
  • 🕵️ Full incognito support
  • 💾 Local storage (no cloud sync)
