# Your Sincere Tab Keeper - Chrome Web Store Description

## Short Description (132 characters max)
A Chrome extension that limits open tabs with playful maze games - helping you live your best tab life, one maze at a time.

## Detailed Description

**Your Sincere Tab Keeper** is designed to help you reflect on your browsing habits and build a more mindful relationship with technology. By adding a playful maze game when you exceed your tab limit, we help you pause and consider whether you really need that new tab.

### 🎯 Key Features

**Tab Limit Enforcement**
- Set a custom tab limit between 1-10 tabs
- Works with all tab opening methods: Ctrl+T, middle-click, bookmarks, and programmatic opens
- Applies to both normal and incognito browsing

**Engaging Maze Challenges**
- Chrome Dino-inspired aesthetic with smooth gameplay
- Progressive difficulty scaling - each maze gets more challenging
- Solve the maze to access your intended webpage
- Beautiful animations and satisfying completion effects

**Mindful Design**
- No escape hatches or bypass options - commitment to your goals
- Playful notifications when attempting to open additional tabs
- Motivational messages to encourage better browsing habits

**Comprehensive Statistics**
- Track total mazes solved and tabs blocked
- View detailed usage insights and patterns
- Monitor your progress toward mindful browsing
- Export your data for personal analysis

**Full Incognito Support**
- Works seamlessly in incognito mode (when enabled)
- Consistent experience across all browsing contexts

### 🧠 Philosophy

We believe that small friction can lead to big changes. Instead of simply blocking tabs, Your Sincere Tab Keeper turns the moment of reflection into a fun, engaging experience. Every maze solved is a step toward more intentional browsing.

### 🔒 Privacy & Security

- All data stored locally on your device
- No cloud sync or data collection
- Open source approach to extension development
- Minimal permissions requested

### 🎮 How It Works

1. **Set Your Limit**: Choose your ideal tab count during setup
2. **Browse Normally**: Use your browser as usual
3. **Encounter the Maze**: When you exceed your limit, solve a quick maze
4. **Reflect & Continue**: Consider if you really need that new tab
5. **Track Progress**: Watch your mindful browsing habits improve

### ⚙️ Customization

- **Flexible Limits**: Adjust your tab limit anytime (requires solving a maze)
- **Progressive Difficulty**: Mazes get more challenging as you solve more
- **Personal Insights**: Detailed statistics help you understand your patterns
- **Clean Restart**: Difficulty resets when you restart your browser
