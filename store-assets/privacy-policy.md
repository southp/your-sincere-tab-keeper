# Privacy Policy - Your Sincere Tab Keeper

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Overview

Your Sincere Tab Keeper ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Chrome extension.

## Information We Collect

### Local Data Only
The Extension stores all data locally on your device using Chrome's storage APIs. We collect and store:

- **Tab Limit Settings**: Your chosen tab limit (1-10)
- **Usage Statistics**: Number of mazes completed and tabs blocked
- **Timestamps**: When you hit your tab limits (for insights)
- **Installation Date**: To calculate usage duration

### What We Don't Collect
- **No Personal Information**: We don't collect names, emails, or personal identifiers
- **No Browsing History**: We don't store or access your browsing history
- **No Website Content**: We don't read or store webpage content
- **No Location Data**: We don't access your location
- **No External Communications**: No data is sent to external servers

## How We Use Information

All collected data is used solely to:
- Enforce your chosen tab limits
- Display usage statistics and insights
- Provide progressive maze difficulty
- Generate motivational messages

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage Only**: All data remains on your device
- **Chrome Storage API**: We use Chrome's secure storage mechanisms
- **No Cloud Sync**: Data is never synchronized to external services
- **Session Data**: Some data (like difficulty scaling) resets on browser restart

## Permissions Explained

The Extension requests these permissions:

- **`tabs`**: Required to count open tabs and redirect blocked tabs to mazes
- **`storage`**: Required to save your settings and statistics locally
- **`activeTab`**: Required to manage tab focus when limits are exceeded
- **`scripting`**: Required to execute maze completion logic
- **`notifications`**: Required to show helpful notifications
- **`host_permissions: <all_urls>`**: Required to redirect any URL to maze when limit exceeded

## Data Retention

- **Settings**: Persist until you uninstall the extension
- **Statistics**: Persist until you manually reset them or uninstall
- **Session Data**: Automatically cleared on browser restart
- **Timestamps**: Limited to last 100 entries to prevent storage bloat

## Your Control Over Data

You can:
- **Reset Statistics**: Clear all usage data through the options page
- **Export Data**: Download your statistics as JSON
- **Uninstall**: Completely removes all stored data
- **Adjust Settings**: Change your tab limit anytime

## Incognito Mode

When you enable incognito support:
- The Extension works the same way in incognito windows
- No additional data is collected or stored
- Incognito tabs are counted toward your total limit
- All privacy protections remain the same

## Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Updates will be posted with the extension updates.

- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

- Other applicable privacy laws
